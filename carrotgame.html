This was a project built with our pair-programming partners, as part of the DOM unit, and helps us practice using event listeners and object-oriented programming. This is a classic snake game, reimagined as a carrot picking game, but the concept is the same. As more carrots are picked, the speed also increases, making this more challenging the longer you play. Don't hit the sides of the field or run into the carrots you've already picked. Give it a shot!
