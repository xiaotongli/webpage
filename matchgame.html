The objective of the second project within the Udacity Front-End Nanodegree program (Spring 2019 term) is to familiarize students with Javascript and the DOM and to use both to control page content and interactions. Event listeners are used to listen for specific user actions on the page and based on the underlying card content, cards are either matched as successful or hidden again to continue the game. The game is over after all cards are matched.

Font Awesome icons were used for the cards and a dotted Subtle Patterns image is used in the background.
