Penguins are one of the most devoted fathers in nature! After laying the egg, mother penguins need to feed in the ocean to replenish her nutrition levels, and in her absence, father penguins continue to nurture and protect the eggs to keep the baby warm in the harsh winter weather, often starving themselves for months to stay consistently with their young.
